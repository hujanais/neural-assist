IPhone and IPad Installation

  • Open your camera app and scan the QR code above to open the website
  • Tap the "Share" button at the bottom of the screen.
  • Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen." If you don't see it, keep scrolling down.
  • Customize the name of the app if you want like 'Neural-Assist', and then tap "Add."
  • The PWA will now be installed on your iPhone's home screen as an app icon.
  • Tap the app icon to launch the PWA. You can now use the PWA just like a regular app on your iPhone.

Android Installation

  • Open your camera app and scan the QR code above to open the website
  • Tap the three-dot menu icon at the top right of the screen.
  • Tap "Add to Home Screen."
  • Customize the name of the app if you want like 'Neural-Assist', and then tap "Add."
  • The PWA will now be installed on your Android phone's home screen as an app icon.
  • You can now use the PWA just like a regular app on your Android phone.

Startup Guide

In order for this app to connect to the OpenAI servers, you will need to create an OpenAI ApiKey.
Again we remind you that this APIKey is local to your device and we have absolutely no access to it.
  • Go to OpenAI
  • Sign-up/Login
  • After logging in, click on your user profile icon on the upper right toolbar.
  • Select View API Keys and +Create new secret key
  • Make sure you write this secret key down because you will not be able to see it again
  • Tip, you can always create new ones if you need to but better to write down the one you created.
  • Enter this apikey into the app and never let anyone see this key!
  • Privacy! We do not have access to your key, it is stored locally on your device
